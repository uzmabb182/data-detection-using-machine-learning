Supervised Machine Learning Model Application Using Flask

By Mubashira Qari

Predicting the Likelihood that Someone will be Arrested or Brought to Court After Being Stopped by a Police Officer

The Data

The data used to train for suspect's summon issued prediction algorithm is an open source, available on NYPD-New York City Police Department.

The datsets are used for educational purposes only. The outcome of the suspect's summon issued algorithm may not align with the outcome of the specific suspect.

The Data Processing Steps


The 2017, 2018, and 2019 Stop, Question, & Frisk Datasets are Merged for Training Data
EDA, Preprocessing, Binning, Numeric Conversion, Data Normalization is Performed for Training Data
The 2020 Stop, Question, & Frisk Dataset is Prepared for Testing Data

Data is Fitted to the Model for Prediction and Accuracy Score is Calculated
